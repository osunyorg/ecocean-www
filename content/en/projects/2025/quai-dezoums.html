Installation of artificial habitats as part of the renaturation project for the new pier Quai Dezoums.

body: >-

Partners and funders


The construction of the new Dezoums pier led to the implementation of compensatory measures aimed at restoring fish nurseries. As part of this effort, Ecocean assisted Eiffage Construction in installing Biohut artificial nurseries.

«  The implementation of the compensatory measure at the Quai Dezoums redevelopment site in Port-Vendres was carried out by the Ecocean team. This work, conducted in consultation with the scientific committee and led by Eiffage Civil Engineering, proceeded smoothly in terms of timing, adapting to the project’s progress, and the installation of the Biohut®.  »

 
Maxence Morel
Environmental Officer – Ecologist, Eiffage Construction

The project in pictures

Ecocean
Installation of 96 Biohut poles along the entire length of the Dezoums pier. 

Ecocean
Each pole, located next to the pier, is equipped with a set of four structures positioned at two different heights to accommodate a variety of species.

Biohut dikes have also been installed at the base of the pylons, providing suitable habitats for various species throughout the water column.

Ecocean
Two banded bream  (Diplodus vulgaris)  and Hervia  (Cratena peregrina)

Common octopus  (Octopus vulgaris)
