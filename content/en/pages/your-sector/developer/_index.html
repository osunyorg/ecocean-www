Are you undertaking a development project or construction work in a port or aquatic environment, and would you like to help promote biodiversity? We can offer you our expertise.

body: >-

Supporting you

We support you at every stage of your development and infrastructure projects, offering practical, scientifically validated solutions and providing comprehensive support right through to completion.


Initial contact and project analysis
Whether in response to a tender or for a project in the design phase, we analyse the technical characteristics of the development (structures, phasing of works, site constraints) as well as the environmental and regulatory context. This initial stage enables us to identify environmental challenges and potential areas for action.

Ecological and functional assessment
An environmental assessment is carried out to characterise existing habitats, current biodiversity and the ecological functions that need to be preserved or enhanced. This analysis considers the interactions between the project, the aquatic environment and the future uses of the structure.

Co-designing ecological solutions
Working alongside your project management and engineering teams, we co-design solutions tailored to the project: the integration of ecological habitats, avoidance, mitigation or compensation measures, and ecological engineering features incorporated into the structures. The solutions are designed to be compatible with the technical, scheduling, and financial constraints of the project.

Costing and integration into the construction project

A detailed cost estimate is carried out to incorporate environmentally friendly solutions from the construction phase onwards. This enables costs to be anticipated, tendering processes to be streamlined, and environmental measures to be integrated into the project's schedule and budget.

On-site installation

We are involved during the construction phase to implement the selected environmentally friendly solutions. Our work is coordinated with the site teams to ensure compliance with the construction schedule, safety regulations, and operational constraints while minimizing the impact on the environment.

Suivi écologique et valorisation du projet

Environmental monitoring programs can be set up to assess the effectiveness of the measures implemented and to document the results achieved. The data collected can be used to highlight the project's environmental performance to project owners, regulatory authorities and the public.

Ensuring the project’s long-term viability

Once the project is complete, we support the transition to the operational phase of the structure. The solutions implemented are designed to be sustainable, compatible with the infrastructure's intended use and, where appropriate, integrated into a circular economy approach.

« The implementation of the compensatory measure at the Quai Dezoums redevelopment site in Port-Vendres was carried out by the Ecocean team. This work, carried out in consultation with the scientific committee and led by Eiffage Civil Engineering, proceeded under optimal conditions in terms of timing, addressing the constraints of the project’s progress, and the installation of the Biohut®.  »
Maxence Morel
Environmental Officer - Ecologist, Eiffage Construction
