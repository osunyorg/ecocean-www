Are you involved in the renewable energy sector (offshore wind, floating solar) and looking to adopt a proactive environmental approach? We work with you to develop innovative, scientifically sound solutions to improve the health of the aquatic ecosystems associated with your infrastructure.

body: >-

Supporting you

We get involved right from the early stages of your project, working closely with your teams responsible for environmental and impact assessments. We aim to provide you with optimized technical solutions to enhance your artificial infrastructure in aquatic environments.


Initial assestment
You describe your project to us, and we evaluate its environmental challenges and opportunities for improvement.

Joint development of actions
We work with your teams to identify best practices for aquatic environments, tailored to your specific context (mitigation, compensation, or ecological enhancement).

Regulatory support
We collaborate with you to draft the technical and scientific documentation required for your permit application, drawing on our field experience.

Economic analysis
We provide a detailed, fully transparent cost estimate for the selected solutions.   

Operational implementation
Our teams carry out the underwater work to deploy the approved solutions (support or mitigation measures).
