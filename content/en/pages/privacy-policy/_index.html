Traffic measurement

Audience tracking is provided by  Plausible , an open-source, privacy-friendly solution. The solution is hosted in Germany (Hetzner) and operated by a company in Estonia. Audience tracking does not use cookies.

Cookies

The site uses the  Osuny  content management system, which does not use cookies. However, cookies may be used by third-party services. Among these services, some video platforms may set cookies despite an explicit request not to do so. Other services, particularly those integrated via iframes, have their own cookie policies.
