Founded in 2008, the Professional Union of Ecological Engineering (UPGE) brings together more than 150 professionals in the field of ecological engineering (construction companies and engineering firms) who work to restore the functions of living systems. To achieve its goal of structuring the sector, the UPGE coordinates its network of members, develops tools for recognizing the skills of ecological engineering professionals, and represents the sector before numerous institutional stakeholders.
